Relaxing at Kolab Reservoir

Kolab Reservoir is a peaceful retreat, surrounded by rolling hills and greenery. The blue waters reflect the sky, creating a magical view.

Popular for boating and birdwatching, Kolab is an eco-tourism spot that attracts nature lovers and photographers year-round.

A picnic by the lake or a boat ride at sunset makes Kolab an unforgettable destination in Koraput.
