A Day at the Tribal Market

Koraput’s weekly tribal markets are a cultural treasure. Local tribes gather to trade vegetables, spices, jewelry, and traditional crafts.

The markets are full of life: colorful attire, rhythmic tribal songs, and handmade artifacts showcase the region’s heritage.

For visitors, it’s not just shopping—it’s an immersion into the vibrant traditions of Odisha’s tribal communities.
