Hanuman Temple

The Hanuman Temple at Damanjodi is a local spiritual centre where devotees gather for vibrant festivals and daily rituals.

Visitors are welcomed by the temple's simple but powerful architecture and the warm devotion of the community. Festivals here are colourful and energetic.

It's a good stop for travellers curious about regional temple traditions and village life in Koraput district.
