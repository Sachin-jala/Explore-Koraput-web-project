Panchapatamali

Panchapatamali is an intriguing local hill with cultural significance and scenic lookouts over the Damanjodi valley.

The place blends nature and local myth; small shrines and rock formations add character to short hikes and sunset visits.

Perfect for travellers who enjoy quick treks and learning the stories that local elders share about the landscape.
